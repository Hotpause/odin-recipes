Ingridients

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 and 1/2 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 1 and 1/2 cups granulated sugar
  • 4 large eggs
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla extract
  • 1 cup buttermilk

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to the recommended temperature for your pizza crust.
  2. Roll out the pizza dough to your desired thickness and shape.
  3. Spread a generous layer of the rich tomato-based sauce evenly over the dough.
  4. Sprinkle a premium blend of cheeses, ensuring even coverage.
  5. Add your favorite fresh vegetables, such as bell peppers, mushrooms, and onions.
  6. Distribute savory meats like pepperoni, sausage, or grilled chicken across the pizza.
  7. Sprinkle aromatic herbs and spices for added flavor.
  8. Bake the pizza in the preheated oven until the crust is golden and the cheese is melted and bubbly.
  9. Remove from the oven, let it cool for a moment, then slice and enjoy your delicious homemade pizza!
